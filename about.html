Our Story

Rhino Security & Armed Response was founded in Kuruman in 2013 with a single goal: making our community safer. Registered as The Rhinos Security and Armed Response (reg. K2013184224), what started as a local armed response operation has grown into a regional security group with branches serving Kuruman, Kathu, Postmasburg, Upington and Danielskuil.

From our head office at 5 Hobson Street, Kuruman, our 24-hour control room coordinates armed reaction officers, guards and patrol vehicles across towns, townships, farms and industrial sites — because out here, response time is everything.

We combine trained people on the ground with modern technology, including Olarm smart alarm systems and a dedicated radio network, so our clients get fast response backed by reliable monitoring wherever they are.
