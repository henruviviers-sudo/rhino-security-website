Locally Owned, Locally Trusted

Rhino Security & Armed Response was founded in 2013 with a single goal: making the Kuruman area safer. What started as a local armed response operation has grown into a full security group serving the John Taolo Gaetsewe district, with a presence in both Kuruman and Kathu.

We combine trained, on-the-ground officers with modern technology — including Olarm smart alarm systems — so households, farms and businesses get fast response backed by reliable monitoring.
